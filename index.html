About Us

Here at Vanna's Beauty Salon, we want to inspire people to express their beauty in every way.
Our staff is highly qualified with constant training. We have prepared a rich offer of cosmetic services and enable you to escape from everyday stress in a warm and friendly environment. An individual approach and a professional and educated team will help you truly indulge in the pleasure and time you have given yourself.

Vanna's Beauty Salon was founded in May 2019 and in a short time after its opening, it resulted in numerous satisfied customers. We are located in a quieter place just 20 minutes away from Dublin City Center.
